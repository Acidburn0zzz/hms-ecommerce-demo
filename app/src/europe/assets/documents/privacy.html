HMS Core Shopping Demo Privacy Statement

Update: May 2021

HMS Core Shopping Demo is a demo application developed by Huawei Software Technologies Co., Ltd. to guide developer to use HMS Core capability in E-Commerce industry application. HMS Core services will be used in the demo like Huawei Account, Location Service, In-App Purchase and so on.

During the demo usage, we will collect the account related information. Your information will be stored in the server in Germany.

1. How we will collect and user your personal information

【Product Visual Search】

Product visual search function can search the same or similar product in the configured product list according to the product picture. This function is included in the shopping demo.

To provide product visual search function, the following information will be collected:

• The product picture you have taken will be uploaded to the server to analyze and find the same or similar product.

Your product picture will not be stored in the system.

【Application Usage Information】

During the application usage, the system will collect the App usage information. It will help developer to operate the application and show the usage statistics in Huawei Analytics service.

The following information will be collected:

• The page visit statistics in the Demo, product purchase information, product favorite information, HMS service usage information.

Device identity (AAID), demo package name, demo version, exception log, interface usage information, network type, device type, country, OS version.

This information will be used for the in-app data analyze. It will be stored for maximum 6 months since it is collected.

【Feedback and Kit Favorite】

If you have suggestion on the demo, you can send us your comments and suggestions in the feedback page. If you like the function in the demo, you can click the “Like” button to save it.

To provide this function, we will collect the data below:

• The favorite kit list information.

• The suggestions and score you send in the feedback page.

Your suggestions will be used in the future version. Kit favorite information will be used to analyze the HMS Core service usage information in E-Commerce industry. Your account name and avatar will not be collected. The data will be stored for maximum 6 months since it is collected.

【Location Service】

Location service will be used in product page. It is used to show the virtual delivery address. It shows how to get your position and use it in shopping demo.

The following information will be collected:

• Location information, including anonymous IP address, approximate location information, the country code in SIM card.

This information will be used to be shown in the application, but it will not be stored in server.

【Voice Search Function and Translation Function】

When you use voice to search product information, system will upload your voice to server to make analyze.

When you use translation function in comment page, system ill upload your comment content to server to make translation.

Your personal information will not be upload to server.

The voice and comment content will not be stored in server.

【Scan Shopping and Scan Pay】

When using Scan Shopping, system will scan the bar code to get the product ID and go to the related product page.

When using Scan Pay, system will scan the QR code and jump to the payment succeed page.

The bar code and QR code is analyzed locally, it will not be stored or upload to server.

【Bankcard Scan】

When you make payment for the product, you can choose bankcard payment. System will scan the bankcard and get the bank card number, Issuer and other information to show in the demo.

The bank card information is locally analyzed. It will not be stored or upload to server.

【Push Service】

Huawei Push service is used to push message to your phone to receive the notification from demo. To simulate the order payment and other notification information, the demo integrates Push service. To build the communication between your device and server.

To provide push service, the following data will be collected:

• Device information, like device ID, device model, ROM/EMUI/Push service version and user ID.

• Network information, like network connection type, MMC, MNC and IP address.

This information is used to build connection. It will be stored maximum 1 year after you deactivate the demo application.

2. Device Permission

Location permission: When you open the demo, you can enable this permission. We will show the virtual offline shop on the map according to your current position. When you enter the product page, we will get your position and show it in the delivery area. If you do not need to use this service, you can close the permission. It will not affect other function.

Microphone permission: When you use “Voice Search”, you can enable this permission. We will recognize your voice to generate the search content. If you do not need to use this service, you can close the permission. It will not affect other function.

Camera Permission: When you use “Scan Shopping”, “Scan Pay”, “Bankcard Scan” and “Product Visual Search”, you can enable this permission. System will take picture via camera to get the product information, scan card or scan code. If you do not need to use this service, you can close the permission. It will not affect other function.

Local storage permission: When you use “Scan Shopping”, “Scan Pay”, “Bankcard Scan” and “Product Visual Search”, you can enable this permission. System will get picture in local storage for the product information, scan card or scan code. If you do not need to use this service, you can close the permission. It will not affect other function.

You can manage the application permission in the “Setting” menu.

3. What Are Your Rights and Options

You have the following data subject rights and options regarding your personal data:

【Access your data】

You can request information and a copy of your personal data that we have collected and stored in relation to HUAWEI ID by going to Settings > Account center > Privacy center > Request your data on your device.

For additional access requests, please contact us.

【Rectify your data】

In order to keep your data up-to-date and accurate, you can access and modify your data by going to Settings > Account center on your device.

【Port your data】

You can port the personal data in your HUAWEI ID that you have provided to us in a commonly-used machine-readable format by going to Settings > Account center > Privacy center > Request your data on your device.

【Erase your data】

You may at any time:

• Remove your HUAWEI ID account data, which is voluntary for the use of HUAWEI ID, such as nickname, profile picture, security email and security phone number.

• Delete your HUAWEI ID by going to Settings > Account center > Privacy center > Delete account on your device. As a result, your personal data will be erased, including your personal data in other services and from all your Huawei devices (e.g. Huawei smart watch) which are linked with your HUAWEI ID.

• Object to the processing of your data. As a result we shall erase the data that falls within the scope of your valid objection if we no longer have legal basis to continue processing the data.

• Contact us if you think the processing of your personal data is unlawful and your data should be erased.

【Withdraw your consent】

If you have opted to receive electronic direct marketing from us, you can withdraw your consent by going to Settings > Account center > Privacy center > Receive messages on your device.

If you have authorized non-Huawei apps to access your account information when you are logged in with your HUAWEI ID, you can revoke such authorization by going to Settings > Account center > Privacy center > Control account access on your device.

【Object to processing】

If you want to object to the processing of your data for analytics, development or sales and marketing purposes, please contact us.

When making the request, please specify the scope of the request and provide us with the email address or phone number that you used to log in to your HUAWEI ID.

We will contact you to verify your identity in order to proceed with your request.

【Restrict processing】

If you want to restrict the processing of your personal data, please contact us. You have the right to restrict the processing of your data in the following cases:

• Your data is unlawfully processed but you do not want to erase it.

• You have a legal claim that you need to establish, exercise, or defend, and you requested us to keep your data when we would not keep it otherwise.

• You have contested the accuracy of your personal data and the accuracy of your data is pending our verification.

• Your request for objection is pending our verification.

When submitting your request, please specify the scope and basis of the request and provide us with the email address or phone number that you used to log in to your HUAWEI ID.

We will contact you to verify your identity in order to proceed with your request.

【Recover HUAWEI ID access】

You can recover your HUAWEI ID access by going to Settings > Account center > Account security > More > Security center > Change account on your device. This feature allows you to change the phone number, email address, security phone number, or security email address that you use to log in to your HUAWEI ID in case they become unusable.

We will ask for more information to verify your identity in order to proceed with your request.

We will retain your personal information for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Statement, and delete or anonymize your personal information beyond the time set out below, except as otherwise required by applicable laws and regulations.

In the event that we discontinue our product or services, we will notify you in the form of a push notification, announcement, or by other means, and delete or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable period of time.
