Statement About HMS Core Shopping Demo and Privacy

Last updated: May 20, 2021

HMS Core Shopping Demo (short as "Demo") is a service provided by Aspiegel SE, a subsidiary of Huawei incorporated in Ireland (hereinafter referred to as "we", "us", or "our"). Demo is a sample application to guide developer to use HMS Core capability in E-Commerce industry application.

This Statement describes:

1. What Data Do We Collect About You?

2. How Do We Use Your Data?

3. How Long Do We Store Your Data?

4. How Do We Share Your Data?

5. What Are Your Rights and Options?

6. How to Contact Us?

7. How Do We Update this Statement?

1. How we will collect and user your personal information?

As part of the Demo we shall collect and use your personal data only for the purposes stated in this Statement. Personal data refers to any information that enables us to identify you as a user.

We shall collect and process the following data:

• Account information: such as your account name, shipping address and phone number.

• Device information: such as device identifier, country/region code, language setting, device type and name, operating system version.

• Shopping information: such as product browsing history, shopping history, favorites, location for offline shops, and the comments you made.

The information above is stored on client side only to simulate the real online shopping situation.

• Statistics information: such as IP address, login/logout, functions you visit, and the APIs/SDKs usage.

To provide you with the service, Demo requires following permissions to work properly. The “Location” permission is required to provide location based offline shop service. The “Microphone” permission is required to provide the voice search function. The “Storage and Camera” permissions are required to provide the “Scan Shopping”, “Scan Pay”, “Bankcard Scan” and “Product Visual Search” functions. You can manage the permissions in your device settings.

2. How Do We Use Your Data?

We use your personal data in order to provide you with the Demo service and to fulfill the contractual obligations, so that you can use your Demo. This also requires us to process your data for support and communication, conducting consumer surveys, customer complaint handling, maintenance, software and system updates, user identification as well as for problem diagnosis and fixing.

The above mentioned processing of your personal data is based on your contract with us.

In addition, we use your personal data for the following purposes based on legitimate interests pursued by us:

• Analytics and development purposes. This will enables us to understand our users' needs and improve the quality and user experience of our current and future services and offerings. You can object to such data processing as described in Section 5.5 below.

3. How Long Do We Store Your Data?

We will retain your personal data for no longer than is necessary for the purposes defined in this Statement.

We will process and retain your data for the duration of the customer relationship. When you delete the demo, personal data related to your account and associated services, such as the Demo, will be erased. However, we shall retain the following data:

• The products will be kept in your shopping carts or favorite list until you removed them.

• Your payment and network information for twelve (12) months from the date the transaction was made.

• Personal data processed for customer relationship management such as data subject requests, complaints for up to six (6) years from the last interaction.

• Personal data processed for analytics and development purposes as well as for sales promotion and marketing purposes for up to twelve (12) months from the date of collection, unless you object to such processing.

• Data backups and app logs processed to ensure the security of your data and services for up to six (6) months from the date they were created.

Once the retention period expires, we will delete or anonymize your personal data in due time, unless otherwise required by applicable laws and regulations.

4. How Do We Share Your Data?

We store your data in the databases located in the EU/EEA.

We share your data in the following ways:

• If you are using service by logging in with Demo when you are traveling outside of the EU/EEA, your Demo information may be routed through the local site of a Huawei group company operating under contract and acting on behalf of us.

• When required in response to a legal process or request from a competent authority according to applicable laws or in connection with a legal proceeding or process.

• When required as part of a merger, acquisition, sale of assets (such as service agreements) or transition of service to a Huawei group entity or another company.

When transferring and disclosing your data outside the EU/EEA, where the local law may not provide the same level of protection, we comply with applicable legal requirements for providing adequate safeguards to such transfer by incorporating the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC). If you want to obtain a copy of the actual signed SCC, please contact us.

5. What Are Your Rights and Options?

You have the following data subject rights and options regarding your personal data:

5.1. Access your data

You can request information and a copy of your personal data that we have collected and stored in relation to Demo by contact us.

5.2. Rectify your data

You can modify your shopping cart and favorites by yourselves.

5.3. Port your data

You can port the personal data in the Demo that you have provided to us in a commonly-used machine-readable format by contact us.

5.4. Erase your data

You may at any time:

• Delete the products from your shopping cart or favorite list.

• Object to the processing of your data, as detailed in Section 5.5. As a result we shall erase the data that falls within the scope of your valid objection if we no longer have legal basis to continue processing the data.

• Contact us if you think the processing of your personal data is unlawful and your data should be erased.

We shall erase or anonymize your personal data without undue delay in accordance with the retention periods detailed in Section 3.

5.5. Object to processing

If you want to object to the processing of your data for analytics, development or sales and marketing purposes, please contact us.

When making the request, please specify the scope of the request and provide us with the email address or phone number of the Huawei Account ID that you used to log in to the Demo.

We will contact you to verify your identity in order to proceed with your request.

5.6. Restrict processing

If you want to restrict the processing of your personal data, please contact us. You have the right to restrict the processing of your data in the following cases:

• Your data is unlawfully processed but you do not want to erase it.

• You have a legal claim that you need to establish, exercise, or defend, and you requested us to keep your data when we would not keep it otherwise.

• You have contested the accuracy of your personal data and the accuracy of your data is pending our verification.

• Your request for objection is pending our verification.

When submitting your request, please specify the scope and basis of the request and provide us with the email address or phone number of the Huawei Account ID that you used to log in to the Demo.

We will contact you to verify your identity in order to proceed with your request.

6. How to Contact Us?

You can find guidance on how to exercise your data subject's rights under Section 5. If you have any questions, comments, or suggestions on your rights as a data subject or the processing of your personal data by us or if you want to contact our Data Protection Officer, please contact us.

Our address: Aspiegel SE, 1F, Simmonscourt House, Ballsbridge, Dublin, D04 W9H6 Ireland. Registration number 561134.

If you believe that we are not processing your personal data in accordance with this Statement or the applicable data protection laws, you can lodge a complaint with the data protection authority in your country/region, or the Data Protection Commissioner in Ireland.

7. How Do We Update this Statement?

We encourage you to regularly check for the latest version of this Statement from the app settings page as we may update it from time to time. In the event of material changes to this Statement, we will notify you by means of notification dialogs, push messages, emails, and so on, depending on the nature of the change.
